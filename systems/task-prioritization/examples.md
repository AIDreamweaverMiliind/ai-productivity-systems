# Example: Task Prioritization in My Workday

## My Task List

On a typical day, I may have tasks like:

- Respond to emails  
- Prepare a report  
- Attend meetings  
- Follow up with team  
- Review data  
- Update trackers  

---

## Step 1: Categorizing Tasks

### Important + Urgent
- Prepare report (deadline today)

### Important + Not Urgent
- Review data  
- Improve process  

### Not Important + Urgent
- Some emails  
- Follow-ups  

### Not Important + Not Urgent
- Minor updates  

---

## Step 2: Selecting Priorities

I choose:

- Prepare report  
- Review data  

These are the most impactful tasks.

---

## Step 3: Execution

I start my day by focusing on:

1. Prepare report  
2. Then move to review data  

Other tasks are handled later.

---

## Result

- Important work gets done first  
- Less stress during the day  
- Better clarity on what matters
