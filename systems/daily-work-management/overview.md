# Daily Work Management System

## Problem

In my daily work, I often had multiple tasks in my head with no clear starting point. 
I would switch between emails, Excel sheets, and messages without a structured plan. 
Even after working for hours, I felt like I had not completed important work.

## Why This Happens

- No clear system to start the day  
- Tasks are not organized  
- Everything feels urgent  

## Outcome

This system helps me:

- Start my day with clarity  
- Know exactly what to work on  
- Reduce confusion and random task switching
