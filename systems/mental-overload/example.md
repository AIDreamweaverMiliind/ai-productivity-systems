# Example: Reducing Mental Overload in My Workday

## Situation

On some days, I feel overwhelmed with:

- Multiple pending tasks  
- Follow-ups  
- Deadlines  
- Messages and emails  

My mind keeps jumping from one thing to another.

---

## Step 1: Brain Dump

I take a notebook or open a document and write everything:

- Complete report  
- Reply to emails  
- Follow up with team  
- Update trackers  
- Review data  

---

## Step 2: Clear Mind

Once everything is written down:

- I feel less pressure  
- I don’t have to remember everything  

---

## Step 3: Move to System

I then:

- Move tasks into my task system  
- Prioritize them  
- Decide what to work on first  

---

## Step 4: Focus

I pick one task and start working.

---

## Result

- Less stress  
- Better focus  
- More clarity on what to do next
