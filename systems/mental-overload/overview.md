# Mental Overload System

## Problem

In my daily work, I often had too many things in my head at the same time.

Tasks, follow-ups, responsibilities, and pending work would keep running in my mind continuously.

Even when I was working on one task, I was thinking about 5 other things.

This led to:

- Constant stress  
- Lack of focus  
- Difficulty in completing tasks properly  

---

## Why This Happens

- Too many open loops in the mind  
- No system to capture thoughts  
- Trying to remember everything  

---

## Outcome

This system helps me:

- Clear my mind  
- Reduce stress  
- Focus on one task at a time
