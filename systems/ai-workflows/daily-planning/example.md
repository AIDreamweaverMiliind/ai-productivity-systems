# Example: Using AI for Daily Work Planning

## My Task List

On a typical day, I may have tasks like:

- Prepare report  
- Respond to emails  
- Follow up with team  
- Review data  
- Attend meetings  

---

## Prompt I Use

"Act as a productivity assistant. Organize the following tasks into:
- High priority
- Medium priority
- Low priority

Also suggest a simple time-blocked plan.

Tasks:
- Prepare report  
- Respond to emails  
- Follow up with team  
- Review data  
- Attend meetings"

---

## AI Output (Simplified)

### High Priority
- Prepare report  
- Review data  

### Medium Priority
- Follow up with team  

### Low Priority
- Respond to emails  

---

## My Final Plan

- Morning → Prepare report  
- Midday → Review data  
- Afternoon → Follow-ups  
- Evening → Emails  

---

## Result

- Faster planning  
- Clear priorities  
- Less confusion at start of day
