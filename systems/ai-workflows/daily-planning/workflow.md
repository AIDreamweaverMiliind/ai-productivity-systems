# AI Workflow: Daily Work Planning

## Objective

Use AI to organize daily tasks and create a structured work plan.

---

## Step 1: List All Tasks

I write down all my tasks for the day.

---

## Step 2: Use AI to Organize

I use ChatGPT with the following prompt:

"Act as a productivity assistant. Organize the following tasks into:
- High priority
- Medium priority
- Low priority

Also suggest a simple time-blocked plan.

Tasks:
[Paste tasks here]"

---

## Step 3: Review AI Output

I review the output and:

- Adjust priorities if needed  
- Make sure important tasks are included  

---

## Step 4: Execute

I follow the structured plan created with AI support.

---

## Key Learning

Using AI helps me:

- Save time in planning  
- Get a structured view quickly  
- Reduce decision fatigue
