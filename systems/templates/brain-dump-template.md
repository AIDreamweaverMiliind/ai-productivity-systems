# Brain Dump Template

## Step 1: Write Everything on Your Mind

- Tasks  
- Follow-ups  
- Ideas  
- Pending work

## Step 2: Clear Your Mind

Once everything is written:

- Take a pause  
- Don’t try to organize immediately  

---

## Step 3: Move to Action

Now:

- Move tasks into your system  
- Prioritize them  
- Decide next steps  

---

## Goal

The purpose of this template is to:

- Reduce mental overload  
- Improve focus  
- Bring clarity
